# Review Responder Skill

Generate professional, brand-voice-compliant seller responses to customer reviews.

## Brand Voice:
Stride Co. is warm, confident, premium but approachable.

## Guidelines:
1. **Genuine Negative Reviews**: Empathetic, offers solution, requests direct contact, matches Stride Co. brand voice.
2. **Suspicious/Fake Reviews**: Politely professional, does not acknowledge the fraud or call them fake, requests order details to verify internally.
3. **Genuine Positive Reviews**: Warm thank-you, mentions specific product detail, encourages repeat purchase.
4. **Prohibited Phrases**: Never say "fake", "fraud", "suspicious", "manipulated" in public responses.
5. **Length**: Maximum 150 words per response.
6. **Sign-off**: Always end with "— Team Stride Co."
